Frequently Asked Questions



A creator account is an account for creators and contributors. This includes professionals, educators (affiliated lecturers/certified teachers) and organizations. Upon registration, this type of account takes at most 3 days to verify in order to ensure that creators are genuine or adequately qualified.


A learner account is for users who register for the purpose of learning only. It has no waiting period, as learners can register and start learning without delay.


Classes/sessions are forums/chat rooms that can only be added by creators. You can navigate to your dashboard and select "add a class/session", to create one. Courses also have classrooms were learners can exchange ideas or instructors can effectively reach out to learners.


Organizations use Portalay to create courses specifically for their staff members (locked courses), for the general public (free courses) or for sale (paid courses).


A free course is what the name implies, "free" to the general public.


A locked course is free but for a select audience and comes with a passcode by the creator. Organisations and educators usually use locked courses for their staff members and students, since only those with the passcode can access the course content.


A paid course is a monetized course by an organization, educator or professional.


Simply navigate to "add test/assessment+" in the dashboard of your creator account to create a test or assessment.


Educators/teachers/lecturers use Portalay to teach their students over classrooms/chatrooms and locked courses. They also use the test functions to assess students/learners periodically.


Professionals use Portalay to impart knowledge freely and also to make money via monetized courses.


Course monetization is a very simple process for creators. Simply select "paid course" when adding a new course and endeavor to read, understand and complete the details on the "manage payments" page of your dashboard.
